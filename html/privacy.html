PRIVACY POLICY

Your privacy matters to us

Welcome to Anis Boutique. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email, phone number, shipping address, billing address, payment information
  • Usage Data: IP address, browser type, device information, pages visited, session duration

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and deliver your orders
  • To provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • To send order updates, notifications, and promotions
  • To analyze usage and improve website performance

3. Sharing of Information

  • Payment gateways for transaction processing
  • Logistics partners for order delivery
  • Legal authorities if required by law

4. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to maintain sessions, manage cart state, and gather analytics. You may disable cookies in your browser, though some features may not function properly.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security practices. However, no digital system is entirely immune to risks.

6. Delivery

  • Orders are typically processed within 24–48 hours after payment
  • Delivery timelines may vary based on location and courier services
  • Tracking details will be shared via email or SMS

7. Cancellations & Refunds

  • Orders can be cancelled only before dispatch
  • Refunds are issued for failed payments or undeliverable orders
  • For requests, email: anisboutiqueofficial@gmail.com

8. Your Rights

  • Access and update your personal information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of promotional communications

9. Children’s Privacy

Anis Boutique is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

10. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Any changes will be reflected on this page.

11. Contact Us
